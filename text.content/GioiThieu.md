The First International Hospital in Hanoi & Northern Vietnam
With a comprehensive range of International-standard specialist medical services, internationally-trained doctors, a state-of-the-art new hospital facility and advanced equipment, Hanoi French Hospital is widely acclaimed for the “First Class Healthcare” that our patients deserve. Hanoi French Hospital (HFH) was the first and only international hospital in Hanoi and Northern Vietnam when it opened in 1997. Today, it remains one of the leading international hospital serving local and foreign patients from the entire Northern Vietnam region with its new, state-of-the-art facilities, up-to-date medical equipment and most importantly, internationally trained doctors and healthcare professionals, able to communicate in multiple languages. For over 28 years, Hanoi French Hospital has been dedicated to delivering “First Class Health care” for those who live and work in Hanoi and Northern Vietnam. Located in the central Dong Da District, Hanoi French Hospital offers a full spectrum of specialised medical care, fully consistent with international standards of safety, and quality as well as Vietnamese regulations, all in an integrated international style hotel-hospital facility for the best possible patient care."
